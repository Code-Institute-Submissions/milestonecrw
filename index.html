What do we Teach?

We offer tuition in grappling, taking a base from catch wrestling and incorporating techniques from other grappling arts e.g Brazilian jiu-jitsu sambo and judo.This allows us to have a grappling system where we are comfortable at differnt levels and rule sets. We also teach Scottish backhold wrestling a traditional Scottish martial art and traditional boxing use techniques from past greats.

Why choose us?

Located in a great facility in the heart of edinburgh (see links to our main gym page for the other great classes on offer) We teach in a non traditional way (no bowing required here). Classes and PT's are taught by experianced approachable instructors who strive to help everone improve in a enjoyable manner.
